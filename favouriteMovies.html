The Best Movies According to Gustavo

My best 3 movies of all time

Pulp Fiction

Absolute favorite movie ever, in my opinion, the best apperarences of
John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson, Bruce Willis, Wing Rhymes and Uma Thurman ever!!!

Guardians of the Galaxy 1

I repeatedly watched this daily for a long while. When you don't get tired of the
same movie everyday it means it's that good.

The Lord of The Rings(Trilogy)


It has to be the trilogy, because one movie makes such a good connection to the
next one that it feels something is missing if you don't watch the whole trilogy.
